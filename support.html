Support the Series


This concert series depends on you, our audience. With no paid staff and very low overhead, a gift of any size travels a great distance in this project, helping us do everything from paying stipends to the musicians and art spaces to renting chairs and staging and printing the programs. Our skilled volunteers, loyal and growing audience, and your donations have collectively kept our ticket prices the lowest in town. Thus, our concerts are accessible to all, including students who can attend for free. Your attendance at our concerts and your generous donations are our backbone. Thank you!

Please send donations to:
The Downtown Chamber Series
P.O. Box 2288
Phoenix, AZ 85002

Interested in volunteering? Wonderful! Please call us a leave a message so we can contact you: 602-254-1491.

Please note: The Downtown Chamber Series does not have non-profit 501(c)(3) status and therefore cannot offer a tax exemption for your donation. Within our simple model we are able to keep expenses and ticket prices low without formal non-profit classification. If you have any questions or concerns, please contact us!
